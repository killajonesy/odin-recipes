Sliced Grapefruit

Grapefruit is delicious. I know some may not agree, but it's a tasty fruit and I enjoy it every day. This recipe is the most basic one I'm aware of. If you're inexperienced with kitchen work, ask someone to help with cutting the grapefuit.

Ingredients

  • A chef knife
  • 1 Ruby Grapefruit

Steps

  1. Fetch knife from drawer
  2. Rinse grapefruit and place on a cutting board
  3. Slice grapefuit into wedges
  4. Devour the goodness of the grapefruit
