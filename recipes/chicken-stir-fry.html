Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables

I usually do a chicken stir-fry for one of my weekly meals. Something about washing and cutting vegetales for this meal is relaxing. I tend to drink a pantload of coffee while prepping for this meal as it's early in my day and I really need the mental boost to get my carcass in gear.

Ingredients

  • 6 large chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1 large green bell pepper, thingly sliced
  • 1 large red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 22 medium button mushrooms, sliced
  • 6 stalks of celery, sliced
  • 3 cups of jasmine rice
  • olive oil
  • rice wine vinegar
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. In large skillet over medium heat, add 1tbsp of olive oil and chicken.
  2. When chicken is cooked, drain fat and add 1/2cup each of rice wine vinegar and soy sauce. Cook for 5 mins, remove skillet from heat and set chicken aside.
  3. In a rice cooker, add 3 cups of water and 3 cups of jasmine rice and cook.
  4. In large wok over medium heat, add 2 tbsp of olive oil. Let oil heat for about 30 seconds.
  5. Sautee mushrooms in wok until 30, stirring often.
  6. Add diced onion and sautee for 5-7 mins.
  7. Add bell peppers and celery to wok, stirring occasionally.
  8. When peppers begin to soften, add 1/4 cup each of rice wine vnegar and soy sauce.
  9. When vegetables are cooked, make a bed of rice on plate and add vegetable mixture topping with chicken.
  10. Enjoy!
