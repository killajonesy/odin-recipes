Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables

I usually do a chicken stir-fry for one of my weekly meals. Something about washing and cutting vegetales for this meal is relaxing. I tend to drink a pantload of coffee while prepping for this meal as it's early in my day and I really need the mental boost to get my carcass in gear.

Ingredients

  • 6 large chicken breasts
  • 1 large green bell pepper
  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 22 medium button mushrooms
  • 6 stalks of celery
  • 3 cups of jasmine rice
  • olive oil
  • rice wine vinegar
  • soy sauce
